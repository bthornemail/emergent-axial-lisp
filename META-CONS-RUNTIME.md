# META-CONS-RUNTIME.md

## Status

Defined model with Pass 1 implementation of structural CONS only.

## Implemented

Structural `CONS` constructs ordered pairs. `CAR` and `CDR` recover the left and right coordinates of a pair.

## Not Implemented

Resolver CONS, quasigroup CONS, coproduct CONS, centroid CONS, reflection, module loading, target lowering, and effect mediation are deferred.
